The Story of The Indian legacy

From Peak to Cup

Hailing from the mist-shrouded Wuyi Mountains, where tea cultivation is a generational art, our Emperor's Golden Needle was once a tea reserved for royalty. It grows on steep, rocky cliffs, absorbing a unique mineral terroir.

Plucked only during a few days in early spring, our masters select only the most tender, unopened buds, still covered in fine, silvery-gold down. This "first flush" captures the plant's most potent and delicate flavors. The buds are then withered, dried, and sorted by hand, ensuring that only the most perfect needles make it into your cup.
