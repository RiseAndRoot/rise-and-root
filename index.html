About Rise & Root

Rise & Root, founded by Biotechnologist Apurva Dwivedi, was born from a passion for authentic, natural wellness. We journey to the world's most renowned tea regions to source leaves of exceptional quality and character, ensuring a real, authentic taste in every brew. Our dedication to ethical sourcing and traditional craftsmanship means we share only the most exquisite and luxurious teas with connoisseurs around the globe.
