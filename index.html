How to Brew

The art of the perfect cup.

Each tea has its own temperature, its own timing. Follow these steps and the leaves will do the rest.

01
Heat the water

Darjeeling: 85°C — never boiling. Assam & blends: 95–100°C. Use filtered water for best flavour.

02
Measure the leaf

One rounded teaspoon (2–2.5g) per 200ml of water. Adjust to your strength preference.

03
Steep with intention

Darjeeling: 2–3 minutes. Assam & Heritage: 3–4 minutes. Do not oversteep.

04
Strain and savour

Pour through a fine strainer. Take the first sip as is — before milk or sugar — to appreciate the origin.
